## Gemini Added Memories
- **Document Structure Guideline**
    - Use the following structure as a basis when creating or refining documents:
        1.  **Introduction:** Describes the overall purpose and background of the document.
        2.  **Abstract:** Provides a high-level summary, offering a cross-cutting perspective that isn't limited to individual component details.
        3.  **Component Details:** Subsequent sections (from 3 onwards) should delve into each component individually.
